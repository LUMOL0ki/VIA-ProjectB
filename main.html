We invite you to the pleasant environment of the PORT restaurant stylized in the maritime theme. Come and taste our specialty smoked pork knuckle or other great dishes that we will be happy to prepare. Restaurant PORT organizes social events: - family celebrations - corporate parties - funeral feasts - the restaurant can seat up to 80 people We can not only close the restaurant for you, but we always adapt the opening hours to your wishes. And why just organize social events with us? It is not only great meals that we will prepare, but also the availability of the restaurant. You can reach us by train, bus or car. - Havířov train station is located near the restaurant. - about 100m from the restaurant is a bus stop. You can use both public transport Havířov and Ostrava connections. Name of bus stop: Šumbark, bazén - and of course there is parking
